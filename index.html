FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is 'Hash Code'?

Hash Code is a team-based programming competition organized by Google for students and industry professionals (18+) across Europe, the Middle East and Africa. You pick your team and programming language, we pick a real-life engineering problem to solve. Are you up for the challenge?

What is this 'hub' thing?

We're hosting a small event where you can meet up with others taking part in the Online Qualification Round on the 11th February 2016, and compete with each other in the same room. No food will be provided.

Can I attend?

You can attend if you're over 18, and you get a ticket from us. (Plus, you also need to register with Google!
